Welcome to the SDG and
Agenda 2063 Data Portal for Africa

The Portal aims to serve as a unique access point to timely and reliable information about SDG and Agenda 2063 data for Africa. The site is designed to help policy makers and enable the general public to reuse data. The Portal provides easy access to an ever wider range of data from governments institutions and governments partners bodies. You may use and reuse this data for profitable or non- profitable purposes. It will assist national and regional discussions on where each country stands with regards to achieving the 2030 Agenda for Sustainable Development and the African Union 2063 Agenda, as well as which metrics might be useful to track progress.

Data Portal Team

The data portal is managed by the SDGC/A team. The Sustainable Development Goals Center for Africa is deeply committed to accelerating the implementation of the SDGs across the continent. We work with governments institutions and their partners to standardize data and make them more easily accessible. Our goal is to give as much visibility as possible to these data.
As a rule, you can reuse the data for free, as long as you mention the source (see disclaimer). A small number of data are subject to special conditions of reuse, most of which are related to the protection of the intellectual property rights of third parties.
